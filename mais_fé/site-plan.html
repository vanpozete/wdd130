Mais Fé | Talks

Here at my Mais Fé we are constantly working to bring the gospel of Jesus Christ to people's lives through social media. That's why we are pleased to launch this webinar. During this event, our professionals at Mais Fé will share what they do and the best practices to spread the word of God on different types of social media. We will walk you through how to work with the algorithm, how and where you can find inspiration to create fresh and fantastic content, the best tools to use in your platforms, and how to structure a good team to be successful. This event is exclusive and free. So if you want a seat in the front row, take advantage of the opportunity to be with us.

About the speakers

Daniel Costa is our Team Lead Manager and has more than six years of experience with digital marketing, team leadership, and internet culture. He is a specialist in paid ads (Facebook Ads, Google Ads, and others), social media marketing, content generation on different platforms, online strategies, landing pages, design, and video editing. His work is focused on growth and data analysis in various segments such as financial companies, startups, eCommerce, non-profit organizations, colleges, and others. Knowledge in people management, monitoring of KPIs, goals, and strong analytical skills.

André Silverio is the Video Manager at Mais Fé and a video editor with high skills in content editing. He knows the best techniques to work and boost different types of video content. He works with strategies to decide what is the best to work on YouTobe, Instagram, and other social media platforms.

>

Marie Sunaga is our Content Manager and has more than three years of experience creating content for different social media platforms. She is a specialist in advanced SEO ranking techniques and planning social media and blog content according to a target audience. Marie is also a UX Writer and has translated and written content for over three years.

Natasha Iurc is Design Leader at Mais Fé and produces art for social networks, illustrations, and webtoons. She is also an animation producer and has developed filters and graphic materials. She has been working on developing the identity of campaigns for social media. Among her skills are: Graphic Design · Web Design · Design · Illustration · Digital Illustration · Adobe Photoshop · Adobe Illustrator · Adobe XD · User Experience (UX) Design · Animator.

Caroline Vasselai is graduated in Advertising and Marketing from the Pontifical Catholic University of Paraná and graduated in the Pathway Worldwide program from Brigham Young University. She has experience in customer service, briefing creation, media planning, content production, social media, brand relationships with consumers, event organization, communication, presentation, and group management. Today she is Head of Community and Social Media in Mais Fé and interacts directly with our public through videos and other types of content.
